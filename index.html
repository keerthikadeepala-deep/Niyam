SEC 01THE PROBLEM

Quality lives in Excel until the auditor's visit is announced.

Since the Medical Device Rules, 2017, every device sold in India needs a licensed manufacturer with a working QMS. For most small manufacturers, that means a scramble before every CDSCO or Notified Body audit — and silence in between.

₹2–8 lakh

Typical spend on consultants to prepare documentation before a single audit — repeated every cycle.

Every device

MDR 2017 brought all device classes under CDSCO regulation. Thousands of manufacturers are newly accountable for ISO 13485-aligned quality systems.

1–3 people

The size of most quality teams carrying document control, CAPA, complaints, training and audits — largely on paper.

SEC 02WHAT PRAMAAN DOES

An AI co-pilot for your quality head — with a human signature on everything.

Pramaan drafts, tracks and audit-proofs your quality system. Your team reviews and approves. The audit trail records both.

MODULE / SOP STUDIO

Your documents, upgraded

Upload your existing SOPs and quality manual — PDF, Word, even scans. Pramaan maps them clause-by-clause to ISO 13485 and MDR 2017 and drafts what's missing for your review.

MODULE / SHADOW AUDITOR

Continuous inspection

Pramaan scans your records the way an inspector would — overdue training, unclosed CAPAs, unsigned documents — and ranks findings by severity before anyone official does.

MODULE / CAPA

CAPAs that close on time

Guided intake from the shop floor with photos, AI-scaffolded root cause analysis, drafted action plans and effectiveness checks, and escalations before deadlines slip.

MODULE / COMPLAINTS & VIGILANCE

Triage in minutes

Every complaint assessed for reportability, linked to batches and CAPAs, with vigilance reports drafted for your sign-off — nothing falls through.

MODULE / AUDIT BINDER

One-click evidence pack

When the audit is announced, assemble the complete evidence package — documents, records, trends, training files — in an afternoon, not three weeks.

INCLUDED / VALIDATION PACK

Validated out of the box

Pre-executed IQ/OQ documentation and PQ templates ship with your subscription, so the software itself stands up to scrutiny.

SEC 05BUILT FOR REGULATED WORK
